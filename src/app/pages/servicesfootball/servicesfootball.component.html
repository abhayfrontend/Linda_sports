OUR SERVICES


We provide a wide range of services including, but not limited to:
1) REPRESENTATION: Management of the sporting careers of professional footballers including contract negotiations, transfers and mentoring as well as working closely with their families
2) BROKERING: We operate as intermediaries on behalf of players or football clubs that work with us to complete deals.
3) BRANDING AND P.R. With our in-house branding and graphic design specialist, we offer brand development services, media training and image advice.
4) LEGAL ADVICE: We offer both legal support and assistance and work with a number of reputable law firms.
5) FINANCIAL ADVICE: Offering tax advice, financial planning and general financial advice.
6) DIGITAL MEDIA: We help to enhance the image of our clients in the world of social and digital media. Offering in-house services and through our various specialist partners along with management and assistance with regards to communication with the media.
